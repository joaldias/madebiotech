Consultancy

MadeBiotech provides consultancy services in several scientific and technological areas like biotechnology, chemical engineering, environment and environmental educationas well as energy and biomass valorization.

Our services include:

· Environmental education, evaluation of environmental impacts and environmental related communication (including surveys to access public awareness and sensitivity)

· Identification and evaluation of innovative technological solutions for new and old problems and mediation/negotiation with the owners of intellectual property and patents

· Conception and supervision of R&D projects, including sourcing of financial support, preparation of applications and negotiation with potential strategic partners

· Economic evaluation, development of business models and market studies for new technologies and technology-based spin-off’s

· Technology transfer

· Patent applications

If you think we can somehow be useful to you, please contact us. A simple brainstorm can bring bright ideas...
