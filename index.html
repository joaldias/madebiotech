Welcome to Madebiotech

Welcome to Madebiotech, a R&D company with headquarters in the beautiful Island of Madeira, Portugal. Our activities are mainly focused on biotechnology and process engineering and we work to develop sustainable processes for the valorization of natural resources. Our goal is to prove that it is possible to conciliate progress and sustainability. Enjoy our website!

Company

  • Mission

    The mission of MadeBiotech is to develop and to promote the concept of Sustainable Biorefinery for different types of biomass, using processes with low environmental impact and high technological content.

  • Vision

    Madebiotech aims to become a reference company in R&D for processes and products of natural origin for cosmetics, food and pharmaceuticals.

  • Values

    The main value of MadeBiotech is the human potential. A company strongly dependent on R&D activities can never forget that the solutions and ideas wich it sells will always come from the collaborators.

  • Projects

    MadeBiotech is partner in Portuguese and International R&D projects in particular due to its extensive background in the development of methodologies to extract and purify value added compounds from natural matrices
