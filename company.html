Our Company & Mission

Developing green technology alternatives for a sustainable bio-based economy.

The Biorefinery Mission

The mission of MadeBiotech is to develop and to promote the concept of Sustainable Biorefinery for different types of biomass.

In our daily lives, several oil derivatives and synthetic products can be replaced by natural alternatives, provided proper technology is available to obtain them at attractive prices and high quality. Bio-based economy is here to stay!

Strategic Innovation

Besides our own projects, we develop research for our customers in our labs and facilities with guarantees of confidentiality and discretion. This solution allows SMEs to innovate and adapt without major capital investment.
