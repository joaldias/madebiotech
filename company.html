Our Company

Driving sustainable biorefinery innovation from Madeira Island since 2011.

The Biorefinery Mission

The mission of MadeBiotech is to develop and promote the concept of Sustainable Biorefinery for different types of biomass.

In our daily lives, several oil-derivatives and synthetic products can be replaced by natural alternatives — provided the right technology is available at attractive prices and quality. The bio-based economy is here to stay!

Strategic Innovation for SMEs

Besides our own R&D projects, we develop research for our customers in our labs and facilities with guarantees of confidentiality and discretion. This approach allows SMEs to innovate, adapt, and implement modern bioprocessing steps without major capital investment.
