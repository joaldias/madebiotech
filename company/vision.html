Vision

We aim to become a reference company in R&D for processes and products of natural origin for cosmetics, food and pharmaceuticals. We are working to develop a brand which will be an international reference of quality, environmental responsibility and consequent entrepreneurship. The success of our company is also measured by the satisfaction of our collaborators, as part of a team which produces wealth without forgetting the respect for the biodiversity and for the planet. In the long term, we also want the community to be proud of our presence. We hope to show the youngsters that intellectual effort and hard work do compensate, and create in them the desire for a rewarding future as a collaborator of MadeBiotech.

Mission

The mission of MadeBiotech is to develop and to promote the concept of Sustainable Biorefinery for different types of biomass, using processes with low environmental impact and high technological content.
